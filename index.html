HOW IT WORKS

The Packaging Problem

Plastic pollution is a global crisis, damaging ecosystems and harming wildlife and human health. Single-use plastics like bags, bottles, and packaging dominate this waste, breaking into microplastics that infiltrate our oceans, soil, and food chain. Packaging alone accounts for 36% of the 440 million tons of plastic produced annually.

In the Netherlands, only 7% of packaging is reused, with the rest piling up in landfills. If current trends continue, the demand for packaging will rise by 17% by 2050, adding to the staggering 82,353 football fields of plastic waste generated each year. Urgent action is needed to meet Dutch sustainability goals of eliminating fossil-fuel-based packaging by 2050. Let's start building a future free from plastic waste today.

Vision

We envision a world where package-free shopping becomes the new standard. Supermarkets have made consuming goods wrapped in plastic incredibly convenient, but this convenience comes at a cost. Recycling alone cannot solve the mounting problem of plastic waste, especially with the projected increase in packaging use. The only sustainable path forward is reducing our reliance on single-use plastics.

By making package-free shopping simple and accessible, we aim to inspire individuals to adopt sustainable habits and embrace a future free from plastic waste. But our vision doesn’t stop there, we’re on a mission to drive systemic change. Through innovative projects and data-driven insights, we aspire to partner with brands, supermarkets, and policymakers to create a retail landscape that prioritizes sustainability, starting in the Netherlands and setting an example for the world.

About Us

We are four AMS Institute graduates driven by a shared passion for building a sustainable future for the Netherlands. Like many, we often relied on supermarkets for our daily needs, but we were shocked to see just how much unnecessary packaging is used in the current system. This realization hit home when we reflected on our busy lifestyles, where visiting markets or package-free stores wasn’t always feasible.

Motivated by this challenge, we created Winkelwagen to make sustainable living more convenient and accessible for everyone. We believe small changes, when made collectively, can have a massive impact. By combining innovation, convenience, and a strong community focus, we’re empowering people to rethink their habits and join us in reducing packaging waste.

Our goal is not just to reduce single-use plastics but to spark a movement. Through our work, we hope to inspire others to embrace a future where sustainable choices are the norm, not the exception.
